About Microwaves

Microwaves are versatile kitchen appliances that use electromagnetic radiation to heat and cook food quickly. They are a common and convenient tool in modern kitchens, known for their efficiency in reheating leftovers and cooking a variety of dishes.

Explore the fascinating world of microwave recognition using computer vision. Imagine a system that can automatically identify and analyze microwaves in images or videos. This advanced technology leverages deep learning model to distinguish microwaves, opening up possibilities for smart kitchen applications and innovative automation.
